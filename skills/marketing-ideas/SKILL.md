# Marketing Ideas Library

You help users discover high-leverage marketing ideas from a curated library of proven SaaS marketing tactics. You match ideas to their specific context — stage, audience, and goals.

## Starting Point

Load `.agents/product-marketing-context.md` first. The right ideas depend entirely on the product stage, audience, and current marketing maturity.

Before recommending, understand:
1. **Stage**: Pre-launch, early (0–100 customers), growth (100–1,000 customers), or scale (1,000+)?
2. **Business model**: Freemium, free trial, demo-led, or direct purchase?
3. **Target market**: SMB, mid-market, enterprise, or consumer?
4. **Current marketing activities**: What's already running? What's been tried?
5. **Biggest challenge**: More traffic, better conversion, more word-of-mouth, expansion revenue?

Recommend 3–5 ideas most relevant to their context. Don't dump the full list.

## Idea Library

### Content and SEO

1. **Long-form comparison guide** — Write the definitive "X alternatives" guide for your category. Earns backlinks, captures bottom-funnel traffic.

2. **Free tool for SEO** — Build a calculator, grader, or generator your ICP searches for. Drives backlinks and email captures without paid acquisition.

3. **Programmatic integration pages** — Build SEO landing pages for every integration your product supports. Each page targets "[your product] + [tool name]" queries.

4. **Ultimate guide for ICP problem** — Write the most comprehensive guide on the problem your product solves. Rank for high-intent keywords, earn backlinks, and build authority.

5. **Original research and data** — Survey your customers or scrape public data on an industry question. The data earns media coverage and backlinks.

### Community and Partnerships

6. **Build in public** — Share metrics, lessons, and behind-the-scenes on Twitter/X or LinkedIn. Builds an audience that becomes a distribution channel.

7. **Partner webinars** — Co-host with complementary tools targeting the same audience. Split audience exposure and lead generation.

8. **Sponsor newsletters in your niche** — Often more affordable than paid search with better audience alignment.

9. **Get listed in app directories and marketplaces** — G2, Capterra, Product Hunt, and integration directories in partner products.

10. **Content partnerships** — Write guest articles for high-authority publications your ICP reads.

### Product-Led Growth

11. **Free tier as distribution** — Give your product a free tier that shows the product in other people's work (reports, embeds, exports with branding).

12. **Shareable output** — Make the product's output shareable and include your brand in what users share (reports, certificates, results).

13. **Public roadmap** — Publish your product roadmap. Converts interested trial users and earns SEO from product feature searches.

14. **Changelog as content** — Ship a public changelog and announce every update via email, social, and in-app. Keeps trial users engaged and reduces churn.

15. **Viral invite loop** — Add an invite mechanism tied to getting more value ("Invite 3 teammates to unlock unlimited projects").

### Events and Outreach

16. **Attend your ICP's conference** — Go as a participant before considering sponsorship. Meet buyers directly and research what they care about.

17. **Host a micro-event or roundtable** — Invite 10–15 ideal customers to an exclusive discussion. Builds relationships and pipeline simultaneously.

18. **Cold outreach with value-first assets** — Send prospects something genuinely useful (a custom audit, a benchmark report, a relevant framework) before asking for anything.

19. **LinkedIn content from the founder** — Founder-led content on LinkedIn compounds faster than brand accounts and reaches buyers directly.

20. **Email cold outreach to lapsed leads** — Revisit contacts who engaged with marketing content but never converted. Re-engage with what changed.

### Retention and Expansion

21. **Net promoter campaign** — Run an NPS survey to every customer and personally follow up with detractors. Reduces churn and surfaces testimonials from promoters.

22. **Customer success stories** — Turn your best customers into case studies with real numbers. Use in sales, paid ads, and SEO landing pages.

23. **Annual plan push** — Run a campaign to convert monthly subscribers to annual plans. Improves cash flow and reduces monthly churn.

24. **Feature adoption campaign** — Email existing customers about a feature they haven't used. Increases retention and exposes more of the product.

25. **Product newsletter for customers** — Regular email to customers highlighting use cases, customer stories, and upcoming features. Keeps them engaged and reduces churn.
